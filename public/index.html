About Me

Hi! I am a final year Undergraduate Student at Duke University double majoring in Electrical and Computer Engineering with a minor in German. I’m applying to PhD programs during the Fall 2024 cycle and look plan to starting my PhD journey in Fall 2025.

My research interests inlcude adversarial machine learning and developing more secure large models. I have the privilege of working with Professor Neil Gong on a benchmark study comparing passive and watermarking methods on detecting AI-generated images.

Additionally, I was fortunate to work with Professor Tingjun Chen in developing an open-source pipeline for generating high-quality signal strength maps based on geographic data for mobile communications.
